# Company Profile: Synthetica B2B (Sample)

## 1. Company Overview
- **Name:** Synthetica B2B
- **Type:** B2B Software & Service Agency
- **Headcount:** 35 employees
- **Leadership Model:** Founder-led
- **Current Phase:** Scaling fast, experiencing growing operational complexity

## 2. Core Operational Challenges
- **Priority Management:** Teams working on too many conflicting goals simultaneously
- **Accountability:** Lack of clear Definition of Done leads to dropped balls
- **Decision Bottlenecks:** Most decisions route through the Founder, slowing execution
- **Siloed Communication:** Friction between Sales, Delivery, and Product/Ops

## 3. Leadership Team
- **CEO / Founder** — Primary decision-maker, needs to shift toward strategic steering
- **Head of Sales** — Focused on top-line revenue, often disconnects from delivery timelines
- **Head of Delivery** — Constantly firefighting to meet client deadlines
- **Head of Product / Ops** — Trying to standardise processes against ad-hoc requests

## 4. Current Quarter OKRs
1. Standardise the hand-off process between Sales and Delivery
2. Implement a weekly leadership loop to align the 4 core leaders
3. Reduce client churn caused by delayed delivery by 15%
4. Define clear ownership (1 task = 1 owner) for all ongoing projects

## 5. Weekly Rhythm
- Weekly leadership meeting currently unstructured — devolves into status updates
- Goal: replace it with a strict weekly steering loop with accountability
